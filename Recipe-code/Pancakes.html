Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or oil
  • vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon nutmeg

Cooking Steps

  1. Mix all dry ingredient in a bowl
  2. In another bowl, whisk egg, milk, and melted butter
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, stir until just mixed.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan and cook untl bubbles form
  5. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown

Prep Time

10 Minutes
