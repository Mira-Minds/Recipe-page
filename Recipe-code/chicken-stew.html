Chicken Stew

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken (cut into parts)
  • 4 large tomatoes (blended)
  • 2 red bell peppers (blended)
  • 1-2 scotch bonnet peppers (blended)
  • 1 large onion (half blended, half sliced)
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1-2 cups vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • salt to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Step 1: Clean and season the chicken Rinse chicken pieces and season with salt, curry, thyme and 1 seasoning cubes Add sliced onions and a little water Boil until cooked. set aside and keep the stock
  2. Step 2: Fry the chicken Heat some oil in a pan. Fry the chicken pieces until golden brown. set aside
  3. Step 3: Prepare the Stew Blend tomatoes, bell peppers, scotch bonnet and half an onion until smooth.
  4. Step 4: Cook the Stew In a pot, heat oil Add the blended mix and tomato paste. Fry on medium heat, stirring often, for about 15-20 minutes until the oil starts to float on top and the sauce thickens.
  5. Step 5: Season the Stew Add curry, thyme, 1 seasoning cube and salt to taste. Pour in the chicken stock, stir and let simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Add the chicken Add the fried chicken into the stew. Stir well and allow eveything to cook together for another 10 minutess.
  7. Step 7: Serve hot Serve with rice, boiled yam, pasta or plaintain.

Prep Time

20 Minutes
