Chin Chin

Ingredients

  • 2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon nutmeg
  • vegetable oil
  • 1 egg

Cooking Steps

  1. Step 1: Mix dry ingredients In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, baking powder and nutmeg.
  2. Step 2: Add butter Add butter to the dry mix. Use your fingers to mix until the texture is crumbly (like breadcrumbs)
  3. Step 3: Add wet ingredients Add milk and egg. Mix and knead until you get a soft, non-stcicky dough Let it rest for about 5-10 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Roll and cut Lightly flour a surface and roll out the dough to about 1/2 inch thick. Cut into small squares or strips using a knife or cutter.
  5. Step 5: Heat the oil In a deep pot, heat enough oil to deep-fry Test with a samll piece- it should sizzle and rise slowly (not too fast or it will burn)
  6. Step 6: Fry until golden brown Fry in batches over medium heat. Stir constantly to ensure even browning. Remove when golden, drain on paper towels.
  7. Step 7: Let cool and serve Chin chin gets crispier as it cools. Store in an airtight container.

Prep Time

10 Minutes
