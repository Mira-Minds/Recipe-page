Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 cup diced vegetbale (carrots, green beans, green peas, cabbage)
  • a cup of vegetable oil
  • liver
  • soy sauce
  • seasoning cubes
  • 2 cup of cooked chicken and shrimp
  • curry and thyme
  • 1 small chopped onion

Cooking Steps

  1. heat oil in a pan, add onions and saute.
  2. add vegetables and meat, stir fry for 3-5 minutes
  3. add cooked rice and soy sauce/seasoning cubes and add salt to taste
  4. stir everything well for 2-4 minutes and serve hot.

Prep Time

10 Minutes
